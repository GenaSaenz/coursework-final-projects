Design Document

Purpose and Summary of Site

The purpose of this website is to provide an easier way for people seeking new venues and/or bands to find both in one place. By providing pictures, information, and links for both venues and bands along with them being divided up based on location in DFW, we have the ability to not only attrack those seeking something new but also provide a place for venues and bands alike to promote and showcase themselves.

Audiences

The primary audience(s) of this site will be music lovers who are in search of a better place to find information and links to multiple venues and bands/artists in a one-stop fashion. The other portion of the primary audience will be the venues and bands/artists themselves as they find that we provide a place to not only market and promote themselves but can also receive feedback from our visitors via our staff. The secondary audience will most likely be the people who are bored or just looking for something different to do some night during the week or weekend.

Background of the Audiences

The background of our audiences will fall in line with their main purpose in visiting our site. The true music lovers will visit us because they can find new and different music, bands, artists and venues to enhance their journey of growing their collection and expanding their minds and palletes. The bands, artists and venues will be in search of a solid place to not only showcase and promote their talent but also to have the ability to get that feedback in return. Both of these audiences are going to be generally on at least an intermediate level of technology and internet savvy level. The happen-by-chance, Google searchers will find that they can not get information to make plans but may find multiple venues and bands/artists that they now want to look into further. This audience will be more of an across the board group as some may be novices with technology and the internet while others are advanced levels in both. Our site is easy to use for a novice level user but advanced enough not to put off higher level users.

Website Message

The message of this website is to encourage and foster a strong local music scence community in DFW by providing a place that not only entices and informs music lovers but bands, artists and venues as well. Its purpose is to bring DFW's local music scene together with the various parts that make up the community as a whole. By placing area specific, venue specific and genre specific information all in one place but in a way that actually combines all three together creating a versitale and reliable source for all parties involved. And the best part is that we provide a platform that gives people the ability to find things that they might not find otherwise due to frustration, lack of time to search mutliple sites and/or plain novice level skills.

Scope

This website provides enough detail and information coupled with links to provide a variety of users the ability to find what they need to make plans, visit a venue, go see and hear new music and even follow them on their individual sites. While we do not have detailed information about each and every band or venue at this time, we do plan to grow the site within the year to give more attention to bands and their events, merchandise, links to tickets and more. We do provide a members area that allows bands/artists and venues to connect, music lovers to receive personalized emails with upcoming events and information about the bands/artists and venues they choose to follow from our memeber's area listings. We will also be adding a live video stream each Sunday evening, beginning in August, that will allow all visitors and members to have a more personal relationship with our staff and the guests on our show. We will not promote, feature, showcase or market for a single area, venue, band or artist.

Goal of Our Site

Our primary goal is to encourage and continue building the local music scene throughout the entire Metroplex and across all genres by providing a knowledgeable and friendly staff who work tirelessly to ensure that any and all information on our site is accurate and correct at all times. This allows for an enhanced experience when searching for the best of the best in DFW, whether it be a venue, a band, an artist or just a place to find ideas for last minute plans. We strive to bring the DFW music scene and its community together by providing a place that offers information, uses its users feedback to better the site and giving a voice to the lesser known but still AWESOME venues, bands, artists all while feeding our fellow music lovers searching minds and ears new fuel for their fires.

Site Navigation

Global and local navigation are provided through links at the top of each page that are consistent in placement and design. There are also several color highlighted words throughout the site that are links to either the Links page, Dictionary.com for a definition, outside websites, or to this document itself. The use of a dropdown button for target link navigation is provided on the Links page. The email graphic is also a link to the Feedback page. And lastly, there are links to each webpage on this site available for use on the Sitemap page.

Organizational Structure

This website uses mostly the Webs information structure but there are also parts that would fall under the Hierarchy information structure and even the linear structure.

The Site Outline

The way I went about creating the outline for this site is pretty much in conjunction with the way the Sitemap page works. I thought about what the primary focus was, what I wanted to present, how I wanted to divide and cover each topic/element and the flow I felt would be most conducive regardless of user level of experience and tech/internet savvy.

  1. Home Page-About Us
  2. Bands/Venues
    1. Our Favorites
    2. A little about the different DFW areas
    3. Bands/Venues specific to Dallas and Deep Ellum
    4. Bands/Venues specific to Fort Worth and Denton
  3. How can people contact us
    1. Our Feedback page
    2. Our Directory page
    3. Our address on the Home page
  4. A place for members to login
  5. A place to find the various band/venue and parking links
  6. This design document which I feel works best at the far right

The outline gave me a good idea of how I wanted to set up the site what would go on each page and where in the navigation order I felt it would work best overall. This in turn gave me an easy way to build my Sitemap page as well.
