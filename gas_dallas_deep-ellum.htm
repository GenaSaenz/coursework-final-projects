Dallas and Deep Ellum Bands and Venues

"Look no further, we have provide some of the best venues and bands that can be found in Dallas and Deep Ellum. You will find plenty of places to check out and bands to enjoy."

The Curtain Club is located in the heart of Deep Ellum. They predominantly showcase local talent but are and have been frequented by nationally recognized bands like Drowning Pool, Soul Asylum, Henry Rollins, Jack Ingram, Bowling for Soup just to name a few. They provide an excellent atmosphere, supreme accoustics and production engineering for their acts, and the vibe is truely that of all for the love of music. We love The Curtain and frequent often, come join us! Check out our links page. to visit their website.

The Balcony Club is located in Dallas where 26 years ago Burke and Jo Barr opened it adject to the late Lakewood Theatre. This beautifully decorated bar has current art deco and accents from days gone by. They offer coctails and an incredible lineup of free music 7 days a week from 5pm-2am. The sexy speakeasy ambiance is appealing to a an eclectic and diverse range of fans. Check out our links page to visit their website.

A Few Artists That Will Entice and Entertain at The Balcony Club include:

  • ✦GB's Magga Orchestra

  • ✦Liz Mikel

Trees opened their doors in May of 1990 and has hosted acts such as Nirvana, Tool, Arcade Fire, Deftones, Pearl Jam, Dave Matthews Band, Erykah Badu and Radiohead just to name a few. But we like to visit them because they showcase local talent from almost every genre. They have recently renovated the venue and upgraded their sound system. This is definitely a favorite of ours and we recommend checking it out. Check out our links page to visit their Facebook page.

Three Links is located in Deep Ellum on Elm Street. While they are not self described as a "Punk" bar it definitely fits. This awesome venue offers a variety of craft beers, top shelf liquors and wait for it..they are partnered with Fuzzy's Taco Shop so you can order from their full menu via your amazing Three Links bartender..how awesome is that?! They open the garage door facing Elm Street to allow that overall atmosphere of life meets music. The acts booked here range from the hottest up-and-coming bands to the hottest local bands with veteran bands doing their thing in the mix. Check out our links page to visit their Facebook page.
