DFW's Local Music Scene Connection Spot

Our Mission Statement

"Connecting music lovers and supporters with the best local bands and venues in DFW, while connecting bands with venues and helping them grow their fan base. All for the love of Music!"

Welcome to Our Community!

We have all had that thought or conversation about making plans for the evening or weekend and trying to figure out the best spots to go hear live, local music and possibly venturing to unknown venues. This is where we come in. You can find local band info, events and venues all in one place for quick and easy planning. We even have links to show where some of the best parking in Deep Ellum is. You can join our community and receive email notification of upcoming events in your selected areas and/or venues, provide feedback and even pass on information about local bands/artists and venues that you feel should be featured on our page. Bands/artists and venues can also become members and provide information to be featured regarding events, pricing, upcoming tours, and links to their website.

All for the Love of Music!

We are proud to support our local music scenes and venues! We want to help you find events for bands you are already supporting and new bands/artists that you might not have found otherwise. We always appreciate feedback regarding shows you attend and pictures you share are often featured to give our community the full experience. We provide information across a multitude of genres so there is something for everyone. We cannot promise you won't have to make hard choices on which awesome show to attend but we can promise that you will have plenty to choose from!
