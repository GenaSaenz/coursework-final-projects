Fort Worth and Denton Bands and Venues

"Look no further, we have provide some of the best venues and bands that can be found in Dallas and Deep-Ellum. You will find plenty of places to check out and bands to enjoy."

Tomcats is a local dive bar that hosts only local and touring up-and-coming bands. The stage is decent sized and the accoustics are great depending on who is working the sound booth. A lot of our favorite bands right now have played Tomcats at least once and a few of them became bands for us to follow after seeing them play here. While there isn't much seating or much to look at the staff is friendly, the music is good, the drinks are cheap and the vibe is electric and fun. We frequent Tomcats regularly in search of new bands to follow. This is a diverse genre venue and we love it! Check out our links page. to visit their website.

The Ridglea Theater is located in West Fort Worth and is known for showcasing local talent for years. They host a variety of non-musical events, such as wedding receptions, quinceaneras, etc as well. The acts that play here are fun and we frequent this venue often. Several bands that we follow play here as well as the other venues we have already mentioned. It's definitely a must visit at least once type of venue. Check out our links page to visit their website.

Billy Bob's is an iconic venue in Fort Worth's historic Stockyards. This venue features local, statewide and nationally recognized country acts. The atmosphere is fun and friendly and perfect for everything from a fun night out to a first date. The mechanical bull riding ring, the billard tables, the dance floor and drinks are all aspects that add to the Fort Worth country vibe that can be enjoyed by just about everyone. It is definitely a place that should be visited at least once and for those of us who love a great time with great music it is a frequent watering hole. We highly recommend this venue if you are a huge fan of Red Dirt Music as they showcase several acts per month. Check out our links page to visit their Facebook page.

The Abbey Underground is an authentic English tavern located on Denton's historic Square. This venue is fun all the way around and they have one of the largest selections of beverages and a fantastic happy hour. Most of the week entertainment is provided by various DJs and open mic night on Saturdays but Thursday and Friday are reserved for local music acts. The diversity of genres here is not that great but they do offer some decent live music from locals. We personally visit this venue for the fabulous speakeasy nights on Wednesdays inclucing classic cocktails and music from the 20's. This venue is worth mentioning and checking out at least once on any night because the atmosphere is fun and the range of music being played by a DJ or otherwise is entertaining. Check out our links page to visit their Facebook page.
