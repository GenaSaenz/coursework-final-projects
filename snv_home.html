Welcome to The Special Needs Village!

We are a community of parents, family, friends, caretakers, paraprofessionals and teachers who have come together to provide information, resources, personal experiences and support to all who need a companion along this incredible journey of caring for our special kids.

As the mother of son with CP, I understand how scary and overwhelming it can be for your child to be diagnosed with something that you may have heard of but really know nothing about. It can be confusing trying to find even basic information and frustrating trying to keep track of all the things that you may possibly need to research. Having other parents who are going through a special needs journey is not only comforting and uplifting but most of the time it is a necessity in order to even decipher the direction to go at any given point.

Each page provides information, an experience, links to resources and/or ways to arm yourself with knowledge, know that you are not alone. There is information on ways that you can provide helpful information/experiences that we can pass along to others so that they might find the path a little easier as well.

Please feel free to bookmark any of the links you choose to visit. We also recommend having a journal handy during your research times online as you can write down things to discuss or ask questions about with your child's care team. You may also find it helpful to write down your thoughts and feelings as you learn more and ponder over the many types of decisions you may need to make in regard to the information you find here or on any of the sites you visit.
