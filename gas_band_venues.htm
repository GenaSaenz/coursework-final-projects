DFW's Local Music Scene Connection Spot

Bands, Artists and Venues

"Dallas, Fort Worth, Denton and Arlington hot spots, bands and artists are highlighted here to help you discover new venues and music, make plans for your weekend, find that great date spot or satisfy that 'what can we do tonight itch'."

Dallas has always played a role in the local and national music scene. The venues and genres represent a multitude of cultures and diversity. We are highlighting some of our favorites, as well as, our most recommended venues, local bands and artists. Deep Ellum is where the majority of music lovers venture when seeking a good time, something new or just following bands/artists they love and support. There are still a few places outside of Deep Ellum that we will provide.


Fort Worth has played a role in the local music scene for a long time but until recently it was often overlooked for venues and local bands/artists in Dallas, specifically in Deep Ellum. Thanks in part to the new interest in rejuvenating certain areas of Downtown, the addition of Panther Island and of course the historic Stockyards, Fort Worth is building up its place in the diverse music scene. Those of us local to Fort Worth are beyond excited to see it grow and be as big in the local music scene as Dallas and Deep-Ellum in genres outside of Country.


Deep Ellum is most certainly a cornerstone in the DFW music scene. From well promoted, nationally syndicated bands and venues to the awesome hidden-gem venues that are as diverse as the metroplex itself. You are bound to run into someone you know locally or even a few famous faces if you frequent as often as we do. There is something for everyone located within the mesmerizing and intoxicating area that is known as Deep Ellum.


Denton is not as well known in the music scene but has recently been making a name for itself with local musicians. The annual Jazz festival held in May is a big deal and is well known and visited by people from all over. UNT has an excellent music program which helps keep the diverse and growing music scene in people's ears and on their lists. While most of the venues are not big or as prominent on "best of" lists, they are interesting and keep us frequenting the area in search of more!


We will display local bands and venues for each of the DFW locations mentioned on this page on the Dallas/Deep Ellum page and the Fort Worth/Denton page. Please check out these pages as they will have location specific venues and bands listed. And each band/venue will have a link to their individual website or Facebook page located on the Links page. Explore the multitude of options and get ready to have a list of places to visit and bands to see that will keep you entertained and full of surprises!
